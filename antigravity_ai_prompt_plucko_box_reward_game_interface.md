# CORE GAME CONCEPT

The gameplay is based on a moving yellow ball.

The yellow ball moves continuously:

- Left to right
- Right to left
- Back and forth

The movement happens across the top area of the white peg grid.

The consumer watches the moving ball and attempts to time their action perfectly.

When the consumer presses a button:

- The moving yellow ball immediately drops
- The ball falls through the peg grid
- The ball bounces realistically between white peg dots
- The ball moves in a zigzag path
- The ball randomly redirects after each peg hit
- Physics-based motion controls the fall
- Gravity behavior feels realistic

The ball eventually lands inside one of the reward boxes at the bottom.

The entire experience should create:

- Suspense
- Anticipation
- Excitement
- Target prediction
- Replayability
- Reward anticipation
